✨ Overview

What Advanced Calendar Does

Advanced Calendar transforms your Jira data into an intuitive, visual schedule that makes planning and tracking effortless.

🚀 Key Features

Key Features

Weekly and monthly views with true hourly timelines, continuous multi-day bars, color-coded statuses, site/location filter, click-to-details modal, and live analytics.

🎯 Use Cases

Use Cases

Perfect for service visits, on-site work, inspections, maintenance, and any time-based schedule in Jira.

📝 Getting Started

Opening Advanced Calendar

1. In Jira, go to the top navigation and click Apps.
2. Choose Advanced Calendar for Jira from the list.
3. The calendar opens, usually in Monthly view by default.

⚠️ Troubleshooting

If You Don't See the App

Ask your Jira admin to install Advanced Calendar for Jira from the Atlassian Marketplace, and make sure you have access to the projects that the calendar uses.

📅 Views

Switching Between Week and Month View

Advanced Calendar has two main views: Weekly view shows an hourly timeline from Monday to Sunday, and Monthly view shows a classic month grid with continuous bars for multi-day issues. Look for the Week / Month toggle at the top of the calendar to switch between them.

🗓️ Navigation

Moving Around the Calendar

Use the Prev and Next buttons to move backward or forward in time. In weekly view, each step moves by one week (Monday–Sunday). In monthly view, each step moves by one calendar month.

🎨 Colors & Icons

Understanding Colors, Icons, and Legends

Each bar represents a Jira issue. Bar color comes from the issue's status category. Visit-type icons come from your Visit Type field. Check the Status Legend for colors and Visit Type Legend for icons.

🔍 Filtering

Filtering by Site/Location

Find the Site/Location dropdown above the calendar. Choose a specific site/location to show only issues from that location. Choose All to see everything again. The analytics cards update automatically when you change the filter.

📋 Details

Opening Details and Working with Issues

Click on a bar to open a details modal with key fields mapped by your admin. Click "View in Jira" to work directly in Jira - you can transition issues, add comments, and adjust dates.

📊 Analytics

Using the Analytics Cards

At the top of the calendar you'll see Total Visits and Busiest Day. These cards update automatically when you switch views, change date ranges, or filter by site/location.

⚙️ Admin

Admin Panel (for reference)

In Jira, click Apps → Advanced Calendar Admin to access the admin panel. Here you can select projects, map required fields (Start Time, End Time, Site/Location, Visit Type), and configure optional fields.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Common Issues

No events showing: Check your site/location filter and verify field mappings with your admin.

Wrong times: Verify Start/End Time fields in Jira.

Multi-day broken: Check Start/End Time covers the full range.

Layout issues: Try a hard refresh in your browser.
